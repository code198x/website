## About This Site

Welcome to Code198x! This site is dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of programming for vintage computer systems and consoles from the late 1970s through the 1980s and early 1990s.

Our goal is to provide resources, tutorials, and insights into the unique challenges and joys of developing software for these classic machines.
